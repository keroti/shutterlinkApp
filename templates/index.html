About Shutter Link

SHUTTER LINK is a web platform designed to bridge the gap between photographers and clients. It provides a space for photographers to showcase their portfolios and allows clients to easily view these portfolios and inquire about photography services. The platform simplifies the process of finding the right photographer for various occasions.
